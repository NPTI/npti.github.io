About Revoluzione

Revoluzione is Annual Techno-Cultural fest organised by NPTI, New Delhi. It is a two day event involving competitions from all fields like singing, dancing, dramatic, quizzing, technical events and lots more. It promises to be a unique blend of cultural and technical extravaganza.
